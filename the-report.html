The report

Here's the result of your session. If the gauge stops between 0 and 50 it means you've watched more the video appearing on the left side of the screen, the violent ones.
Otherwise, if it stops between 50 and 100, it means you've been watching mostly the right part of the screen , meaning that your sight focused more on the erotic content.
It could happen that the gauge could stop under 0 or over 100; in that case, just go back and repeat the test.
